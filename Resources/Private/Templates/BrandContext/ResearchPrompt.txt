STEP 1 — WEBSITE ANALYSIS

Analyze the website at {{WEBSITE_URL}}. Use the fetched website content below when

{{WEBSITE_CONTENT_BLOCK}}

BUSINESS IDENTITY

BRAND VOICE

TARGET AUDIENCE

KEYWORDS / SEO TERMS

COMPETITORS

LANGUAGE & LOCALE
